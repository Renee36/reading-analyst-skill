# Knowledge Network: 8 Domains × 30 Dimensions

## Classification framework

Cross-reference of **UNESCO ISCED** and **Library of Congress Classification (LCC)**, adapted for personal reading:

| Domain | Basis | Coverage |
|--------|-------|----------|
| I. Self-Growth & Life Skills | LCC BF + L + practical living | Personal effectiveness and daily life |
| II. Humanities & Literature | LCC P + UNESCO Humanities | Literary works by genre and region |
| III. Social Sciences | UNESCO Social Sciences + LCC H/J/K | How human society operates |
| IV. Natural Sciences & Technology | UNESCO Natural Sciences + LCC Q/T | Natural world and technology |
| V. History & Geography | LCC C/D/E/F/G | Time (history) + space (geography) |
| VI. Philosophy & Religion | LCC B | Ultimate questions |
| VII. Arts & Aesthetics | LCC N/M + UNESCO Arts | Human aesthetic expression |
| VIII. Business & Career | LCC HB-HJ + professional skills | Career and business practice |

Each domain follows the **MECE principle**: every book maps to one dimension, no major branch omitted, blank areas assessed equally.

## Rating criteria

| Rating | Score | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| ★★★★★ | 9-10 | Exceptional — systematic understanding, multi-book cross-validation |
| ★★★★☆ | 7-8 | Solid — multiple books with reasonable depth |
| ★★★☆☆ | 5-6 | Basic — several related books |
| ★★☆☆☆ | 2-4 | Weak — sporadic exposure only |
| ★☆☆☆☆ | 1 | Blank — no relevant reading |
